Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how OptiLoop ("the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our creative optimisation tool.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Service, we may collect:

  • TikTok Account Data: Your TikTok username, profile information, and OAuth access tokens required to post content and retrieve metrics on your behalf.
  • Engagement Metrics: Public engagement data (views, likes, comments, shares) for content posted through the Service.
  • Generated Content: Text, images, and metadata for carousel content created by the Service.
  • Brand Configuration: Brand names, themes, and preferences you provide for content generation.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Generate and post content to your TikTok account as directed by you.
  • Collect and analyse engagement metrics to optimise future content.
  • Build and maintain a performance playbook to improve content strategy over time.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in a SQLite database on the machine running the Service. We do not transmit your data to third-party servers beyond what is required for TikTok API interactions and AI content generation via the Gemini API.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with:

  • TikTok API: To publish content and retrieve engagement data. Subject to TikTok's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API: To generate text and image content. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties, except as required to operate the Service (TikTok API, Gemini API) or as required by law.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained in the local database for as long as you use the Service. You may delete all data at any time by removing the local database file. You may also revoke TikTok API access through your TikTok account settings.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Revoke TikTok API access at any time.
  • Opt out of the Service entirely.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

10. Contact
